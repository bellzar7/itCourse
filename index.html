Are there courses for beginners??

Yes, we have courses for all levels of training. We offer basic courses for beginners as well as advanced courses for those who already have some programming experience.

Who teaches ProCode Academy courses?

Our courses are taught by experts with many years of experience in leading IT companies such as Google, Facebook and Microsoft. They will share with you not only theoretical knowledge, but also practical skills and real cases.

How is training at ProCode Academy courses?

Learning includes video lectures, interactive assignments, projects, and live webinars with instructors. You will be able to apply the acquired knowledge in practice and receive feedback from mentors.

Does ProCode Academy help with employment?

Yes, we provide career support to our students, including help with resume preparation, interview training and communication with employer partners.

"Starter" package

  • Web development basics course: HTML, CSS, JavaScript basics
  • Course "Introduction to Python programming": basic principles of programming, variables, loops, functions

"Pro" package

$1000

The "Pro" package is designed for those who already have basic knowledge of programming and want to move to a new level. An ideal choice for those seeking to gain in-depth knowledge and prepare for a professional career in IT.
